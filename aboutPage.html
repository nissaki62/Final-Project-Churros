About

This website is all about trying to make it so that people who love churros as much as we do have the ability to share their likings as well as places that they can find their favorite food. People are able to post photos and share restaurants with others who visit this site in order to share what they love with others.

Goals

The overall goals of this website are pretty simple. We want to make it so that people can share information about churros. Users can share information on what they like about the food as well as by using google maps they can be able to find churros stores near them. On top of it all they will be able to view some amazing photos of churros and maybe even post some of their favorite photos from their past.

  • Make a site where people can share pictures
  • Be able to talk with others that share similar interests
  • Make the best churros page on the internet

Mission

The mission of this website is to create a place where people are able to go and share their experiences and love for the food churros with other people that also like the food. We also want to introduce the food to more people as it is not well known and we want to make it so that more people know what churros are as they are missing out if they do not.
